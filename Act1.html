The Prodigy

The Prodigy is a 2019 horror film directed by Nicholas McCarthy, and written by Jeff Buhler. It stars Taylor Schilling, Jackson Robert Scott, and Colm Feore. The plot centers around a child whose disturbing behavior signals that an evil, possibly supernatural being has taken control of him, forcing his parents to investigate whether sinister forces are involved.

  1. Opening Scene (Young Miles): The movie starts with a young Miles Blume, who exhibits strange and violent behavior. In the opening, he attacks a child at school and shows disturbing signs, indicating something is not right.
  2. Parents' Concern (Sarah and John): Miles' parents, Sarah and John Blume, are deeply concerned about their son’s increasingly erratic behavior. Sarah feels that something supernatural may be involved, while John dismisses her concerns.
  3. Miles' Behavior Escalates: As Miles grows, his violent and unsettling behavior escalates. He begins to engage in dangerous activities, including setting fires and showing aggression toward animals and people.
  4. The Psychiatrist (Dr. Alan Stroud): Sarah seeks help from Dr. Alan Stroud, a psychiatrist, who suggests that Miles may be struggling with a mental disorder. However, Sarah believes that something darker might be influencing him.
  5. The Discovery (Possession and Reincarnation): Sarah begins researching Miles' behavior and uncovers the possibility that her son’s soul may have been taken over by the spirit of a deceased man. She learns about a serial killer, Edward Scarka, who was killed years ago and might be inhabiting Miles’ body.
  6. Supernatural Phenomena: Strange and unexplainable events begin to happen in the Blume household. Sarah realizes that Miles’ behavior is being driven by the vengeful spirit of Edward Scarka, not just mental illness.
  7. Miles Kills Again: As the movie progresses, Miles becomes more violent, and his actions lead to the deaths of those around him. At one point, he kills a man who had previously hurt his mother, showing the power of the spirit controlling him.
  8. The Confrontation: In the film's climax, Sarah faces off with the malevolent spirit inside Miles. She does everything she can to save her son, realizing that she must sever the connection between him and the spirit of Scarka.
  9. Final Twist: The final moments of the film reveal a shocking twist. After Sarah seemingly defeats the spirit, Miles’ behavior remains erratic, suggesting that the possession might not be fully over, leaving the story open-ended and ambiguous.
